# Acme SaaS — brand & design stance

Acme is a tool for focused professionals. The product should feel **calm,
restrained, and confident** — not loud, not playful, not dense.

## Stance

- **Restraint over decoration.** No gradients, no drop shadows for flourish, no
  more than two font weights on a screen (normal and semibold). If a screen feels
  busy, remove something.
- **Whitespace separates; borders are a last resort.** Group with space, not
  dividers. A wall of bordered boxes reads as cluttered. Generous padding.
- **One primary action per view.** Exactly one indigo primary button per screen
  region. Everything else is secondary or ghost. Two primaries competing is a bug.
- **Muted by default, color with intent.** Indigo is reserved for the primary
  action. Red is reserved for destructive actions. Don't use brand color for
  decoration.

## Voice (for UI copy)
Plain, direct, lowercase-friendly. "Save changes", not "Save Your Changes Now!".
